### Uiik

At first glance, a Uiik appears to be nothing more than an adorable, fluffy creature—its calico fur, large kitten-like eyes, and tiny frame evoke an overwhelming urge to cuddle it. But those who succumb to its charm soon learn the truth: the Uiik is a nightmare wrapped in innocence. Its wide, toothy maw, capable of unhinging to reveal rows of needle-like teeth, betrays its true nature as a relentless predator.
Uiiks are solitary hunters, stalking their prey with terrifying patience. They mimic the calls of young animals to lure the unsuspecting into their grasp, only to strike with a bone-crushing pounce. Once locked onto a target, a Uiik will not let go, draining the very marrow from its victim’s bones. No one has ever tamed a Uiik, and those who hunt them often find themselves the hunted.

**A Persistence Hunter.** Uiiks are built for endurance. They can go days without food, tracking their chosen prey with single-minded focus. Their eggs hatch without a mother’s presence, and the first hatchling to emerge will devour its siblings, ensuring only the strongest survive. This ruthless efficiency extends to their hunting style—once a Uiik locks onto a target, escape is nearly impossible.

**A Deceptive Killer.** Despite their tiny size, Uiiks are agile and deadly over short distances. Their ability to blend into their surroundings, combined with their innocent appearance, makes them one of the most dangerous creatures a traveler might encounter. Adventurers who underestimate a Uiik’s cuteness do so at their own peril.

---
- **Innocent Appearance:** Creatures that can see the Uiik must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or be **charmed** until the Uiik attacks or until a creature takes damage from it. A charmed creature treats the Uiik as non-threatening.
- **Persistence Hunter:** The Uiik can track a chosen prey for up to 7 days without rest, ignoring the effects of exhaustion from lack of food or sleep.
- **Solitary Predator:** The Uiik does not willingly share its kills and will attack any creature that approaches its prey.

### Actions
- **Multiattack:** The Uiik makes one **Bite** attack and one **Pounce** attack.

- **Bite:** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
  *Hit:* 1 piercing damage + 3 (1d6) necrotic damage. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be **poisoned** for 1 minute as the Uiik’s saliva numbs its prey.

- **Pounce:** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
  *Hit:* The Uiik **grapples** the target (escape DC 15). Until this grapple ends, the target is **restrained**, and the Uiik can use its **Bone Drain** action.

- **Bone Drain:** The Uiik sucks the bones of a **grappled** creature. The target takes 10 (3d6) necrotic damage, and the Uiik regains hit points equal to the damage dealt. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or its speed is reduced by 10 feet until the end of its next turn as its bones weaken.

- **Mimicry (Recharge 5–6):** The Uiik mimics the sound of a young animal (e.g., a kitten, puppy, or fawn). Each creature within 30 feet that can hear the sound must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or be **compelled** to move toward the sound, believing it to be a helpless creature in need.

### Legendary Actions
The Uiik can take 2 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature’s turn.

- **Leap:** The Uiik moves up to 10 feet without provoking opportunity attacks.
- **Bone Crush:** The Uiik deals 5 (1d10) necrotic damage to a **grappled** creature.
- **Vanish:** The Uiik uses the **Hide** action.
